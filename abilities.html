P
Signature of the Visionary
Two damaging spells create the detonation

Damaging an enemy champion primes them. Hitting that champion with another damaging ability completes the signature, creating a delayed area detonation. This is why Hwei wants clean two-spell sequences instead of random single-spell poke.

  • Try to layer crowd control into damage so the second spell is reliable.
  • Passive detonations punish clumped enemies around objectives and minion waves.
  • WE empowered hits can help you keep pressure and mana while setting up follow-up spells.

Expanding slow and delayed burst

Hwei's ultimate attaches to the first enemy champion hit, expands, repeatedly slows nearby enemies, deals damage over time, then bursts. It is strongest when enemies are already crowd controlled or forced through a choke point.

Leveling Order

Current general bot/APC priority
Skill Priority

Max damage first, control second

First max Waveclear, poke, and reliable damage
Second max Lower cooldown on fear, trap, and pull
Last max Utility stays useful with fewer points

Q > E > W is the default bot/APC order. Q gives Hwei the lane control he needs to farm and pressure, while E second makes his defensive and setup tools available more often.

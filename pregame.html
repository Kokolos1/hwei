Summoner Spells

The only recommended combination for Mel
Flash & Teleport Combination Every Game

Flash is the best summoner spell in the game, hands down. Especially on mages which have intentionally low mobility. This is a MUST USE in every single game. No exceptions. With that said, ensure that you don't waste your flash late game for a single kill (unless in a team fight) AND if you burn flash AND die, that is terrible. PLEASE don't waste flash when you will die regardless.

Teleport is the only secondary summoner spell that I run. Other players use barrier & ghost on occassion, but I find telport to be the most reliable in all situations. With that said, these runes (along with exhaust) are definitely viable. Personally though, I find teleport to be such an overpowered summoner spell when you utilize it properly. Watch the video for more details on how to actually use this spell.

Runes

Standard rune page- I run this EVERY game
Why First Strike?

The alternative to First Strike is Arcane Comet. Mel plays like a burst mage, and First Strike gives 7% bonus damage for 3 seconds. This is VERY effective for a burst mage (Mel uses all her spells within this windows). This combined with the fact that we can always hit the enemy first (AND INSPIRATION IS OP ASF), makes it the best

Why Cash Back, Triple Tonic & JOAT?

Cash Back is literally 1300 gold. No brainer unless you want 10MS from free boots (high elo only). Triple Tonic has no alternatives (rest of runes in this row suck). Jack of all trades is 6ish free ability haste. Not great but pretty good. Can definitely be swapped for cosmic insight.

Why Manaflow & Transcendence?

Mel (and most mages) NEED a mana rune. Manaflow band is really really strong, and essentially gives us infinite mana if we play properly. Transcendece is 10 free ability haste and is significantly stronger than gathering storm (due to spiking earlier and more reliable). The only change we might make is Manaflow > Gathering Storm if you find you don't go OOM

Why These Stat Shards?

We are running scaling stat shards in this page. Mages outscale ADCs, BUT they do win lane convincingly. To offset this, we can be greedy with our runes. 8 Ability haste is worse than adaptive force early, but it scales significantly better. We also go health scaling instead of flat since our lane phase should be relatively safe.

Items

Build path and situational picks
Doran's Ring
Every game, no exceptions. Mel cannot make good use of other starting items.
2× Health Potions
Start your first potion at ~180–200 damage taken. Upgrade to Refillable on first or second reset.
Warding Totem
Must purchase on every champion. Swap to Farsight Alteration in the late game.
First reset priority: Spend ALL your gold and do not leave base with 300g unspent. Build toward Lost Chapter first (it builds into all mage starting items and gives mana on level-up). Only buy generic boots before completing your first full item. Video will contain all the details
Sorcerer's Shoes
Default boots in all cases. Buy after 1st item almost every game.
Ionian Boots of Lucidity
Run instead of Sorcerer's Shoes only when the enemy has 2–3 champions with significant magic resist built. These are the only alternative to sorcerer's at the moments.

Mel is a burst mage. The goal to consistently be able to one-shot squishy targets while having enough CDR to kill tanks. The below build will have slight variance depending on the game, please watch the video for full context.

Blackfire Torch
Best first item. Burning effect on every ability landed. Provides the mana needed to sustain expensive spells early.
Cosmic Drive
Second item. Provides ability haste and movement speed on ability hits for better chase/poke and kite potential.
Shadowflame
Third item. Great for shredding shields and maximizing burn damage against targets with healing or shielding effects.
Rabadon's Deathcap
Fourth item. Multiplies total AP by 30% — massive power spike for poke and sustained burn damage.
Void Staff
Fifth item. Build when 2–3 enemies have significant magic resist. Mathematically gold-efficient at ~120 MR on the enemy.
Zhonya's Hourglass
Sixth item. Third or fourth vs burst/engage comps, AD assassins (Zed, Talon), or Malphite. Stasis is the best active in the game.
